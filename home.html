The components of car detailing

Interior detailing: a fresh inside out

The interior of your car is your personal haven. Interior detailing involves deep cleaning the entire interior cabin. Upholstery, carpets, mats, dashboards, windows, doors, door jambs, door frames, door hinges, seat tracks, seatbelts, the headliner, and every other nook and cranny in the center console area receive special treatment. This process isn't just about cleanliness; it's about maintaining and preserving the inside of your car. Kyle's auto detailing service pays special attention to the interior, ensuring every detail is perfect.

  • Comprehensive cleaning: Every surface, nook, and cranny is vacuumed and thoroughly wiped down.
  • Fabric care: Carpets, upholstery, and leather are deep cleaned and treated for stains.
  • Door treatment: Door panels, frames, jambs, and hinges are given expert care and restored to new condition.

Exterior Detailing: shine and protect

Complete mobile auto detailing encompasses more than just the interior; it extends to a meticulous exterior detailing process. Exterior detailing is about much more than just a good wash. It includes paint care through polishing and waxing, as well as cleaning wheels, tires, and all other visible components on the exterior of the vehicle. This comprehensive approach is what achieves that head-turning, glossy finish.

  • Paint protection: Application of wax or sealant to protect the paint and add shine.
  • Wheel cleaning and protection: Wheels and tires are spotless.
