Complete auto detailing services

Striving to far exceed your expectations and deliver the results you expect from a premium detailing experience.

Interior Detailing

Designed to comprehensively sanitize and clean. Floor mats are expertly cleaned and restored to their factory condition. A commercial grade Stihl vacuum is used throughout the vehicle. Door jams, door frames, and door hinges to be thoroughly cleaned. All seats and seat surfaces to be expertly cleaned. Panels, trimmed elements, consoles, dashboard areas, cupholders, buttons, navigation screens, knobs, controls, handles, seat belts, seat tracks, etc. will be meticulously cleaned. Interior glass cleaned to perfection. On average this baseline service takes 4.0 hours.

Exterior Detailing

Process works to eliminate surface contaminants. A rinseless wash using a premium grade solution is used to clean the surface. All four wheels will be cleaned and coated with a specialty surface protectant to maximize appearance and protection. All four tires will be degreased and applied with a water-based tire shine. Other exterior elements and glass will be thoroughly cleaned. A premium grade binding polymer monomer agent will be applied to all exterior surfaces to provide 90 days of contaminant protection. On average this baseline service takes 2.0 hours.

Shampoo Treatment

Cleans, conditions, and restores all carpeted and upholstered areas inside the vehicle. A commercial grade, heated, carpet extractor from Mytee Corporation is used to shampoo and steam all carpeted floor linings and upholstered seats. The machine expels 210 degree pressurized water into the fibers and then immediately suctions the soiled water back into the machine. The Mytee Spyder HP 60 is the gold standard in carpet extraction technologies and is guaranteed to leave your carpets and upholstery looking factory new. On average this add-on service takes 1.5 additional hours beyond your baseline service selection.

Paint Correction

Mechanical 2-step approach intended to remove severe paint imperfections. A coarse cutting compound that maximizes defect removal is used to eliminate heavy swirl marks, scratches, water spots, and other contaminants. The material is called DA-Course and is engineered by Rupes. A fine finishing compound that combines noticeable cutting performance with impressive finishing is used to refine the appearance and condition of the painted surfaces. The material is called DA-Fine and is engineered by Rupes. On average this add-on service takes 4.0 additional hours beyond your baseline service selection.

Surface Protection

Mechanical 1-step approach intended to remove minor imperfections, including light swirl marks, scratches, and other imperfections, while creating a durable layer of protection that provides up to 12 months of protection against environmental contaminants. A finishing polish that combines incredible shine and extremely durable protection is used to layer down a protective coating and shield the vehicle from the elements. The material is called UNO Advanced and is engineered by Rupes. On average this add-on service takes 2.0 additional hours beyond your baseline service selection.

Headlight Restoration

Requires three steps to properly remove water spots, oxidation, scratches, swirls, and yellowness. First, a coarse 400 or 1000 grit sandpaper is applied to the surface to eliminate the imperfections. This process is then followed by application of 2000 and then 3000 grit sandpaper to smooth out the surface. A heavy cutting compound and heavy cutting pad are then used to remove the damage caused by the sandpaper applications. This step begins to bring clarity and consistency to the lights. On average this add-on service takes 1.5 additional hours beyond your baseline service selection.

Contaminant removal

Paint contaminants include a variety of organic and inorganic matter, and are notoriously difficult to strip from a vehicle's painted surfaces. Conventional soapy washes or power washing has proven to be ineffective in addressing these contaminants, such as grains of dirt, grime, asphalt, pollen, iron, etc. Clay bar treatment is a safe and highly effective means for removing these contaminants. Paint decontamination consists of a thorough application of premium, fine-grade clay bar to renew the painted surfaces. On average this add-on service takes 1.5 additional hours beyond your baseline service selection.

Mold Remediation

Addresses mild to severe cases of mold and algae growth. Mold remediation can be a process. In some situations, additional treatments, at no additional charge to you, may be required to effectively eliminate any growths occurring inside the vehicle. Comprehensive interior detailing begins the process and ensures that as much of the growth is removed from the vehicle as is possible. Shampoo treatment using a commercial grade, heated carpet extractor is then used to deeply sanitize and clean the carpets and upholstered sections. On average this add-on service takes 1.5 additional hours beyond your baseline service selection.

Water Spot Removal

Water contains mineral deposits that are left behind once the water evaporates. The longer these deposits are left on the vehicle's exterior surfaces the more difficult it is to safely remove them. Mineral deposits can over time begin to etch into the vehicle's gel coat and break down its structure, effectively ruining the paint's appearance. Removing mineral deposits from painted surfaces and trim requires both a chemical and mechanical response. The chemical approach is used for more intricate locations while the polishing machine and a cutting compound are used for painted panels. On average this add-on service takes 3.0 additional hours beyond your baseline service selection.
